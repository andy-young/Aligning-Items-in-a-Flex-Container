Aligning Items in a Flex Container

box1

align-itemsproperty to align box1 on the cross axis (vertical), justify-content to align the item on the main axis (horizontal)

Properties that control alignment

  • justify-content-- controls alignment on main axis
  • align-items-- controls alignment on cross axis
  • align-self-- controls alignment of individual item on cross axis
  • align-content-- controls space twixt flex lines on the cross axis
